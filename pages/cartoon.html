Cinderella

Disney Cartoon

Based on the fairy tale "Cendrillon" by Charles Perrault,
it is twelfth in the Walt Disney Animated Classics series,
and was released on February 15, 1950. Directing credits go
to Clyde Geronimi, Hamilton Luske and Wilfred Jackson.
Songs were written by Mack David, Jerry Livingston, and
Al Hoffman. Songs in the film include "A Dream Is a
Wish Your Heart Makes", "Bibbidi-Bobbidi-Boo","So This Is Love"
, "Sing Sweet Nightingale", "The Work Song", and "Cinderella".
Soundtrack album by Various artists Released February 4, 1997
